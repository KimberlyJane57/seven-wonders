3. AlUla, Saudi Arabia

Description

AlUla is evidence of 200,000-year-old piece of Arabian history. It's located in the heart of Saudi Arabia's northwestern desert. It's estimated that less than 5 percent of the site has been excavated. The UNESCO World Heritage site of Hegra is famous for its elaborate monumental tombs.
