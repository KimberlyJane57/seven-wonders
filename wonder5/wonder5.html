5. Cappadocia, Turkey

Cappadocia, Turkey

Description

One of the world's natural wonders is Cappadocia. This otherworldy expanse of enormous rock towers, called 'Fairy Chimneys', streaked in red and ochre with wind-sculpted crowns, is one of the most surreal landscapes on Eart. Starting around 1200 B.C. people began carving homes inside the rock itseld. The rock homes of Cappadocia are like works for art, with detailed facades, doors, windows, and staircases hewn from the mountain within. Entire towns have been hollowed out under the ground like inverted skyscapers.
