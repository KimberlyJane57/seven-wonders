4. Tiger's Nest Monastery, Bhutan

Description

Tiger's Nest Monastery or Paro Taktsang is special. Perched on the edge of a cliff 3,000 feet above the Paro Valley, this complex of four Buddhist temples and residential buildings, with whitewashed walls, bright red roofs and golden-domed ceilings, is a true wonder. Located in Bhutan surrounded by Himalayan Kingdoms.
