The Lake District, Great Britain

Description

Spanning 190 miles across Northern England from St Bees on the Irish Sea to Robin Hood Bay on the North Sea, it has grown to become the most popular long-distance hike in the country. The most beautiful part of all is the section which crosses the Lake District.
